Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Selling to East Village Buyers? (And Why!)

Have you ever looked around your apartment and thought, “I have a ton of valuable stuff, but I’m just not using it”? You aren’t alone. At East Village Buyers, we see a massive variety of items and people every single day.

Why do people decide to sell their items? It must be hard to let go sometimes.

It definitely can be, but motivation changes depending on the person! Honestly, most of the time, people don’t want to sell their things—they just need cash. Life happens, bills need to be paid, and you might not have a stash of gold lying around. But what you do have is a pair of high-end sneakers, a luxury handbag, or premium accessories sitting in your closet. Selling those items is a fast, smart way to get the cash you need right now.

Is it always about needing fast cash?

Not at all! We have a completely different clientele who couldn’t care less about the price. They are just fashion-forward people who bought an item, wore it a couple of times, and decided, “Eh, it’s not my style anymore.” Instead of throwing thousands of dollars into the garbage, they call East Village Buyers. They either sell it to clear out closet space, or they trade it for something else we have that actually fits their current vibe.

What if someone thinks they don’t own anything “valuable” enough to sell?
The Golden Rule Every single person has valuable things in their house. It all depends on how you look at it.

Take a look around your room right now:

  • Your Jewelry Box: Look past the perfect pieces. Do you have missing earrings? Broken gold chains?
  • Heirlooms: Vintage jewelry left behind by mothers or grandmothers that you’ll never wear.
  • Electronics: A high-end camera that you bought, used once, and has sat in a drawer for 2 or 3 years. It might feel “old” to you, but it is absolutely still worth money!
Why do so many customers keep coming back to East Village Buyers?

It’s simple: trust and the best prices. Our customers already know how we operate. Once you come to East Village Buyers and make a deal with us, you’ll never go anywhere else. Why? Because our profit margins are very small. We purposely keep our margins tight so we can buy and sell our products for the absolute best prices in the city.
