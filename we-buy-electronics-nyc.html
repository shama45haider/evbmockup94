Apple Audio

Buy & Sell AirPods in NYC

We buy and sell brand-new AirPods, AirPods Pro, and AirPods Max only. If you have a sealed box or an open box that's never been used, bring it in. Used AirPods are not something we purchase — the reason is simple: earbuds are very personal, condition is hard to verify after use, and the resale market for used AirPods is tough.

What We Buy & Sell
  • AirPods (latest generation) — sealed or brand new open box
  • AirPods Pro (latest generation) — sealed or brand new
  • AirPods Max — all colorways, must be brand new or like new sealed
What "Brand New" Means to Us

Brand new means the AirPods have never been paired with a phone and are in original unused condition. The box should be intact. Sealed is ideal. Open box is okay if everything inside is untouched. We'll check the pairing status on the spot — AirPods tell us if they've ever been used before. If they have, we won't be able to make an offer.

AirPods Max — Special Notes

AirPods Max hold their value better than regular AirPods because they're a premium over-ear headphone. All colorways are welcome — Midnight, Starlight, Blue, Purple, and Orange. They must be brand new. Bring the Smart Case if you have it. The original box adds value too. We see a lot of gift AirPods Max that were never opened — if that's you, you're in the right place.

Our Inspection Process for Cameras

Cameras go through the most thorough check of anything we buy. We power it on, fire the shutter multiple times, test autofocus, take a test shot to check the sensor, inspect the LCD for dead pixels, and check all ports. This takes 10–15 minutes and happens right at the counter in front of you.

What Affects Your Camera's Price
  • Shutter count — lower is better, like mileage on a car
  • Sensor condition — dust or damage affects value
  • Body wear — light scratches fine, cracks are a problem
  • Original box, straps, batteries, and charger add value
  • Lenses included with the body increase the offer significantly
Gaming

Buy & Sell PlayStation 5 in NYC

We buy and sell the PlayStation 5 console — disc edition and digital edition. The PS5 is one of the most actively traded consoles in the secondhand market right now, and we see them come through the store regularly. If you have one to sell or you're looking to buy one, come in.

What We Buy & Sell
  • PS5 Disc Edition — standard console with disc drive
  • PS5 Digital Edition — the slimmer, disc-free version
  • PS5 Slim — both disc and digital versions
  • DualSense controllers — adds value when included with console
  • Original cables and power brick — adds value
Condition Requirements

The PS5 must be in full working condition. We test every console before making an offer. Here's what our inspection looks at:

  • Powers on and boots to the PlayStation menu normally
  • HDMI port and video output working — we test with a TV
  • No loud fan noise or overheating signs
  • All USB ports functional
  • Disc drive accepts and reads discs (disc edition only)
  • DualSense controller charges and responds correctly
How to Prepare Your PS5 to Sell

Getting your PS5 ready takes about five minutes and makes the whole process faster:

  • Sign out of your PlayStation Network account from the console settings
  • Go to Settings → System → Reset Console → Full Factory Reset
  • Bring all original accessories — cables, power brick, controller
  • The original box is a bonus but not required
  • Bring your valid photo ID — required by New York State law
What About Games & Accessories

We buy the PS5 console and DualSense controllers. We don't purchase individual games separately — the games market fluctuates too much for us to offer fair prices on individual titles. If you have a bundle with the console and several games, we'll factor in the extras when we look at the whole package. Extra DualSense controllers in good working condition are something we do buy alongside the console.

Buy & Sell iPads in NYC

We buy and sell brand-new iPads only — the latest iPad, iPad Air, and iPad Pro models. Sealed or open-box unused. Walk in and we'll make you an offer on the spot.

Models We Buy & Sell
  • iPad (latest generation) — standard model, WiFi and Cellular
  • iPad Air (latest generation, M-series chip) — 11-inch and 13-inch
  • iPad Pro (latest generation, M-series chip) — 11-inch and 13-inch
  • All storage sizes — 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB, 2TB
  • All colors as released per model
  • Future iPad models as they release
Brand New Only — Why

We only purchase iPads that are brand new — sealed box or open box that has never been set up or used. Used iPads are not something we currently buy. The iPad resale market for used units is competitive and margins are tight enough that we can't offer fair prices on pre-owned iPads. A brand-new sealed iPad, on the other hand, is something we can price confidently and sell quickly.

What "Brand New" Means

Brand new means the iPad has never been powered on and set up with an Apple ID. Sealed box is ideal. Open box is fine if the iPad inside has never been activated — we'll verify this by powering it on. If the setup screen shows Apple's welcome screen with no account linked, it qualifies. If it shows someone's home screen or asks for a passcode, it doesn't.

What Affects Your Price
  • Model tier — iPad Pro gets the strongest offer, standard iPad the lowest
  • Storage size — more storage means a better offer
  • WiFi vs Cellular — Cellular models command more
  • Sealed vs open box — sealed gets a slight premium
  • Apple Pencil or keyboard included — factors into a bundle offer
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase — helpful but not required
