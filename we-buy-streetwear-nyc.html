Supreme

We Buy & Sell Supreme in NYC

Supreme box logos, hoodies, tees, accessories and rare vintage pieces. We authenticate everything and stay current on demand.

Supreme has consistent collector demand and secondary-market value. Box logos especially — we know the eras, printing details, and what's worth real money. Whether it's a classic red box logo or a rare collaboration piece, we authenticate carefully and price fairly. Fakes are common so authentication is non-negotiable.

What types of Supreme do you buy?

Box logos (hoodies, tees, hats, accessories), collaboration pieces, vintage Supreme from the '90s and 2000s, rare releases. If it's Supreme and there's a market for it, we're interested.

Do you specialize in Supreme box logos?

We buy plenty of them. Box logos have strong resale value. We know the printing details, tag variations, and what makes an authentic piece across different eras.

How do you authenticate Supreme?

Tag details, printing quality, material construction, color accuracy, tag placement. We check stitching and whether the piece matches known production details from that era.

Do you buy Supreme collaborations?

Yes. Supreme x Nike, Supreme x Comme des Garçons, Supreme x Louis Vuitton — collabs often have higher value than regular releases. We price them accordingly.

What about vintage and early Supreme?

We buy them. Early Supreme from the '90s and early 2000s can be valuable. Condition and rarity determine the price. Bring them in for an offer.

Does condition matter for Supreme?

It does. Deadstock or minimal wear commands premium. Well-worn pieces have lower value but still have a market. We grade honestly and price accordingly.

What if I don't have the original tag?

The piece itself matters most. Some people remove tags after purchase. We authenticate from the garment — tag helps but isn't the only factor.

Can I sell a mixed batch of Supreme pieces?

Absolutely. We price each piece individually. One rare box logo, one collaboration hoodie, one regular tee — we'll give you an offer on each.

How do I know if my Supreme piece is worth selling?

Bring it in for a free quote. Box logos and collabs are usually worth more. We'll let you know what the current market pays for your specific piece.

Should I clean my Supreme before selling?

Don't use harsh chemicals. A gentle rinse is fine. We're assessing the piece authenticity and condition — leave any aggressive cleaning alone.

Vintage

We Buy Vintage & Rare Streetwear in NYC

90s streetwear, vintage workwear, deadstock tees and rare pieces. We know vintage details and price accordingly.

Vintage streetwear has serious collectors and values can be high when condition and era are right. A deadstock 90s Champion reverse weave, a vintage Tommy Hilfiger, a rare vintage tee from a now-legendary brand — we authenticate and price fairly. Vintage is about era, rarity, and condition. Deadstock is premium; well-preserved worn pieces are still valuable depending on rarity.

What counts as vintage streetwear?

Generally 1980s–2000s pieces, original vintage workwear, streetwear brands from that era, deadstock or well-preserved pieces. The era and rarity determine value.

Do you buy deadstock vintage?

Yes — this is premium. Deadstock '90s hoodies, unworn vintage tees, original tags still on — we pay accordingly when we can authenticate them.

How do you authenticate vintage pieces?

Tag details, fabric content, construction methods, printing techniques — all changed over time. We know the differences between eras and spot inconsistencies.

What about worn vintage pieces?

Still valuable if rare and in acceptable condition. A well-worn '90s piece from an important brand still has serious collector appeal. We grade condition and price accordingly.

Do you buy vintage band tees?

Yes, when they're genuine and from bands with collector following. We assess condition, rarity, band importance, and price fairly.

Do you buy vintage workwear?

Carhartt, Dickies, Levi's — vintage workwear has serious following. Rare colors, deadstock, or well-preserved pieces are valuable. Bring them in for an offer.

How much does the original tag matter for vintage?

A lot. Original tag helps with authentication and appeal. Some people removed tags after purchase. We assess from the piece itself; tag is helpful but not required.

What's the oldest streetwear you would buy?

We handle pieces from the 1980s forward. If it's genuine vintage and it's rare we'll take a look. Come with what you have and let us evaluate it.

Do vintage pieces need to be in pristine condition?

Not necessarily. Deadstock should be pristine but worn vintage pieces can have character. Rarity and authenticity often matter more than perfection. We grade what we see.

Can I sell a mixed collection of vintage and modern?

Of course. We price each piece individually. Vintage gets premium treatment; modern gets standard grading. One visit, all handled fairly.

What designer brands do you buy?

Balenciaga, Givenchy, Vetements, Rick Owens, Raf Simons, Yohji Yamamoto street pieces, and other high-end designers with street-focused collections.

Do you buy Balenciaga streetwear?

Yes. Balenciaga hoodies, tees, and pieces with their distinctive design language. We authenticate carefully since counterfeits exist.

How do you authenticate designer streetwear?

Tag details, fabric quality, construction precision, printing method, serial numbers when applicable. Designer pieces have consistent quality standards; we know what's real.

Do you buy Givenchy and Vetements pieces?

How do designer prices compare to luxury?

Designer streetwear typically has lower resale than haute couture but solid secondary market. Pieces from seasons in demand command higher prices. We price current market fairly.

Can I sell multiple pieces from different seasons?

Absolutely. We evaluate each piece individually based on season, condition, demand, and authenticity. One visit, all handled separately.

Do you buy collaborations from designer brands?

Yes. Designer collaborations often have higher value. Bring them in and we'll authenticate and price fairly.

What if I'm not sure of the exact season or collection?

Bring it anyway. We can identify season details from tag information and construction. We'll authenticate and give you an offer based on what we find.

Condition matters more for outerwear than for some categories, so we grade carefully and explain what we see.

What types of hoodies do you buy?

Supreme hoodies, BAPE hoodies, branded pieces, vintage hoodies, collaborations, designer hoodies. Condition and brand determine value. Rare pieces and early versions command premium.

Do you buy vintage jackets?

Yes. Vintage Carhartt, Dickies, vintage leather, vintage denim, vintage branded pieces. Condition is important for jackets. We grade and price accordingly.

What about collaborative outerwear?

How do you assess jacket condition?

Can I sell a hoodie without the drawstrings?

We can still evaluate it. Missing drawstrings lower the offer but won't kill it. We'll give you a fair price for what you have.

Do you buy rare or limited-edition pieces?

Yes. Limited drops, exclusive releases, rare pieces from brands with cult following — we know the landscape and price fairly.

What about vintage band hoodies or tour pieces?

We buy them when authentic. Band/tour hoodies have collector value. We authenticate from tag details and construction; bring them in for an offer.

How important is original branding or patches on jackets?

Important for authenticity and value. Missing patches or removed branding lower the price. We explain what affects the offer as we assess.

Can I sell oversized or sized-out hoodies?

Yes. Size does affect resale value — some sizes move slower than others. We price based on what the market pays for your size and style.
