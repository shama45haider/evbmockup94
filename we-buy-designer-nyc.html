Before You Come In

  • Authentic pieces only We buy and sell genuine designer goods. Every item is inspected for authenticity before we make an offer. Replicas, counterfeits, and super-fakes cannot be purchased.
  • Good to excellent condition Light wear, corner scuffs, and minor interior marks are fine. Heavy staining, cracked leather, broken hardware, or strong odors reduce the offer — or may disqualify the piece.
  • Photo ID required New York State law — every seller needs a government-issued photo ID. Driver's license, passport, or state ID. No exceptions.
  • Receipts & dust bags help Original receipt, dust bag, box, and authenticity cards aren't required but can strengthen your offer. Bring everything you have — we'll factor it in.

Buy & Sell Louis Vuitton

Louis Vuitton is one of the most traded luxury brands in NYC — and we buy and sell it every week. From classic Monogram and Damier to newer Empreinte and reverse canvas, bring your LV in and we'll make you a fair offer on the spot.

Popular Models We Buy & Sell

What Affects Your Price

Louis Vuitton holds value well, but condition and demand still drive the number. Here's what we look at:

  • Canvas or leather condition — vachetta patina is normal
  • Hardware — zipper pulls, locks, and feet
  • Interior lining — stains and pen marks matter
  • Date code and overall authenticity
  • Dust bag, box, and receipt — bonus when included

Chanel pieces get a close look — authenticity and condition both matter a lot at this price point:
